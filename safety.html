Guest information

Safety

Plan ahead, follow confirmed arrival information and raise any safety concerns before or during your stay.

Before arrival

Read your booking confirmation, arrival instructions, house rules and emergency information. If you have mobility, medical, sensory or access requirements, ask for confirmation before booking.

Fire safety

Holiday accommodation operators in England must consider fire safety duties for small paying-guest accommodation. Guests should review escape routes on arrival, keep exits clear and follow all property notices.

Gas, electricity and appliances

Use appliances only as intended and report faults immediately. Do not cover heaters, overload sockets or use damaged electrical items.

Access note

The public listing states that the bedroom is on the ground floor and that there is a step up into the shower. Confirm any access requirements before booking.

Official guidance

Fire safety guidance for small paying-guest accommodation is published by the UK government: Making your small paying-guest accommodation safe from fire.
