Privacy policy

Last updated: 12 April 2026. This policy covers this website only.

Completion required

The data controller name and correspondence address must be added once the owner/operator details are confirmed.

Who controls personal information

Controller: The Bungalow Burnley owner/operator, details to be confirmed.

What this website collects

This static website does not include a contact form, user accounts, payments, analytics, advertising pixels or newsletter sign-up. It may record basic technical request information through GitHub Pages hosting logs, such as IP address, browser information, requested page and timestamp.

Bookings and third parties

If you email about availability or a stay, the information you provide is used to respond to the enquiry and manage any agreed booking. If this site later links to a third-party booking or payment service, that service will control the information you provide there.

Why information may be used

Hosting logs may be used for security, abuse prevention, troubleshooting and keeping the website available. Direct enquiries, once contact details are added, should be used to respond to the enquiry and manage pre-booking or guest support.

How long information is kept

This website does not keep a customer database. Any future direct enquiry records should be kept only as long as needed for the enquiry, booking administration, legal duties or dispute handling.

Your rights

UK data protection law gives individuals rights over their personal data, including access, correction, deletion, objection and complaint rights, subject to legal limits.

Official guidance

Privacy information should follow the ICO’s right-to-be-informed guidance: ICO right to be informed.
