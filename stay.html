Inside the stay

Bungalow details

A home away from home for two guests, with one-level ground-floor living, a bedroom that sleeps two with a choice of twin or double beds, super-fast WiFi, a fully equipped kitchen, en-suite bathroom, patio and parking. Best suited to three nights or more.

Living space

A simple room made for switching off.

The living room has a sofa, armchair, TV, side tables, soft lighting, framed wall prints and mustard curtains around the main window. It is the place to sit down properly after a day around Burnley, Pendle or the wider Lancashire countryside.

  • Sofa and armchair seating for two.
  • TV set-up for quiet evenings in.
  • Warm lamps and soft furnishings rather than a bare rental feel.
  • One-level ground-floor layout with the main rooms close together.

Rooms

Every part of the bungalow has a job.

The best thing about a small holiday bungalow is that nothing has to be overcomplicated. The rooms are close, practical and clearly set up for two people who want a home away from home for a few nights or a full week.

Bedroom - sleeps two

The bedroom sleeps two and can be arranged with twin or double beds, with bedside tables, warm lamps, blue bedding and a padded headboard. It suits two guests who want a flexible setup rather than a standard hotel room.

  • Twin or double
  • One level

Fully equipped kitchen

The kitchen has the essentials for self-catering stays, with hob, oven, sink, washing machine, worktop space, cupboards and a bright window. It is suited to breakfast, simple dinners and longer-stay basics.

  • Self-catering
  • Kitchen essentials

Dining table

A proper table for two sits in the living space, giving guests somewhere to eat, plan days out or work for a short spell without using the sofa.

  • Seats 2
  • Living space

En-suite bathroom

The en-suite bathroom includes an electric shower, towel heater, sink and toilet. Guests should still check suitability if step-free shower access is essential.

  • Electric shower
  • Towel heater

Patio and arrival

The outside space gives the stay a calmer arrival than a flat above a shop or a hotel corridor. Parking is listed for one car, with the patio close by.

  • Parking
  • Patio

Practical notes

What to check before booking

Access

The bungalow is all on one ground-floor level. Guests with limited mobility should still confirm shower access and any thresholds before booking.

House rules

The stay is no smoking and no pets. Occupancy is for up to two people unless confirmed otherwise in writing.

Included items

Fuel, power, bed linen, towels, super-fast WiFi and kitchen essentials are part of the practical stay. Always rely on the final written confirmation for the contract position.

Arrival rhythm

A quiet base after a Lancashire day.

Arrive, park off-road, settle in and keep the evening simple. Shops, food, larger supermarket options and pubs are nearby, while Burnley's parks, heritage sites and moorland routes make easy day plans.
