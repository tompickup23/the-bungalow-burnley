Inside the stay

Cottage details

A compact, practical base for two guests, with the essentials covered and Lancashire close at hand.

At a glance

One bedroom, one bathroom, simple comfort.

The Bungalow is listed as sleeping two guests in one bedroom, with one bathroom, WiFi, garden space, a shared patio and off-road parking for one car.

  • Ground-floor bedroom.
  • Kitchen equipment including oven and hob, microwave, fridge and freezer.
  • Washer/dryer, iron, ironing board and hairdryer.
  • TV with Freeview, DVD player, DAB radio and WiFi.

Practical notes

What to check before booking

Access

The bedroom is on the ground floor. The listing notes a step up into the shower, so guests with limited mobility should confirm suitability before booking.

House rules

The public listing says no smoking and no pets. Occupancy is for up to two people unless the booking partner confirms otherwise.

Included items

Fuel, power, bed linen and towels are listed as included. Always rely on the final booking confirmation for the contract position.

Arrival rhythm

A quiet base after a Lancashire day.

Arrive, park off-road, settle in and keep the evening simple. Shops, a bakery, larger supermarket options and pubs are listed nearby by the booking partner, while Burnley’s parks, heritage sites and moorland routes make easy day plans.
