The cottage

Ground-floor comfort, tucked close to town and hills.

The Bungalow keeps the stay straightforward: a comfortable bedroom, bathroom, WiFi, kitchen essentials, a shared patio and parking. Burnley is close by, while Pendle Hill, Towneley Park and wider Lancashire routes are easy to reach.

  • One-bedroom accommodation for two guests.
  • Ground-floor bedroom with a step up into the shower.
  • WiFi, TV, DVD, DAB radio and practical kitchen equipment.
  • Shared patio, outdoor furniture and off-road parking for one car.
